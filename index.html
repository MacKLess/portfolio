Current Projects

The following are my previous projects, for your viewing convenience. Author recommendations are indicated by the green text. Enjoy!

  • Rebuilt Wiki Page: (Cowriter: E.Belmonti) Saving the best for last, Belmonti and I used Bootstrap, CSS, HTML, and Git to recreate a Wikipedia page.   • Cherry Blossom Dreaming: (Cowriter: E.Belmonti) An enjoyable page codesigned with Belmonti extolling the virtues of vacationing in Japan during Cherry Blossom season. It worked --now I want to go! In this site, we heavily utilized Bootstrap, CSS, HTML and Git.
